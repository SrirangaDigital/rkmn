The goal of the Advaita Sharada project is to make available our ancient Shastric texts for access via the Internet and other computer-based devices like tablets and smartphones. Leveraging audio, video, commentaries, sub-commentaries, notes, tags and hyperlinks, we hope to provide a platform for in-depth research and additional learning for seekers, scholars and students.

Under the direct guidance of Jagadguru Shankaracharya Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji, the Advaita Sharada project is being executed by Sri Shankara Advaita Shodha Kendram, Narasimha Vanam, Dakshinamnaya Sri Sharada Peetham Sringeri.

The first offering of Advaita Sharada is a text searchable, extensively hyperlinked Internet edition of the Sri Shaankara Granthavali, published by the Vani Vilasa Press, Srirangam.

News and updates

The Guruparampara Stotram of Dakshinamnaya Sri Sharada Peetham, Sringeri. (Learning Version)

On Jan 4, 2015, devotees assembled in Sringeri in large numbers to celebrate the the silver jubilee of Jagadguru Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji's Peetharohanam. At this historic event, the Jagadguru announced His decision, as divinely-ordained by Goddess Sharadamba, to accept Brahmachari Sri Kuppa Venkateshwara Prasada Sharma as His successor-designate.

In a traditional ceremony, the Jagadguru initiated Brahmachari Sri Kuppa Venkateshwara Prasada Sharma into Sannyasa and bestowed upon him the Yoga Patta (monastic name) of Sri Sri Vidhushekhara Bharati. This video contains the Anugraha Bhashanams delivered by the Jagadgurus on the evening of this historic event.
